DateTime

Introduction

Skeleton provides classes that wrap the PHP DateTime classes to provide a more complete and consistent interface.

  • A_Datetime: provides date and time conversion and formatting functionality.
  • A_Datetime_Duration: defines a positive or negative length of time that can be added or substracted from dates.
  • A_Datetime_Range: has start and end dates to allow checking whether a date or other range is in or out of a date range.
  • A_Datetime_Timezone: provides support for getting information about time zones and calculating offsets to other time zones.
